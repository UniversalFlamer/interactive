  • Q: What is the name?

    A: I call it "Tick in a tock".

    It is generally a clock but you may ponder do we need another "clock" to control the timings and sequences? The answer is yes! Just like most digital devices with complicated timings, there is a superior, fatser clock to manipulate everthing! So I guess it is pretty fun to know that it is not only a single clock but a clock inside another clock! The inner clock ticks, and one pixel changes and tick-tock, tick-tock, tick-tock, the similar process iterates and finally you can visually discern it is a pixel clock!

  • Q: What should I do with it?

    A: Well, the refreshing rate is very low (approximately 0.002315Hz for a frame or one pixel refreshes per second.).
